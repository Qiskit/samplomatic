Improved the two synthesizers `RzRxSynth` and `RzSxSynth` to always generate Clifford angles when given a U2 gate that is Clifford. It is only possible to end up with non-Clifford angles when the gate being synthesized is a Z rotation, or a pi-gate of some angle. Either case leads to a degree of freedom in how to distribute the angle between the first and last RZ gate of the synthesis. This was fixed by enforcing the last angle be 0 in both cases.
